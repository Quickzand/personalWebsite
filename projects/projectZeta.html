Project Zeta
Github
The zoomba project has been worked on with a group of friends and I. Zoomba is designed as a real life tomodachi that can map its surroundings and navigate to any point. Most of our work on the project was done during covid, and it acted as an opportunity for us to learn how to interconnect our software with our hardware.
